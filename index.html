Getting There


Airport

Take the subway on the station underneath the airport. The venue is just 3 stops away (stop at Oriente station). You can also get the AeroBus, but takes longer and is more expensive.

Train

If you came by train, then stop at Oriente station and you’re already a stone’s throw from the Venue.


Out of the City Bus

Stop at the Oriente Station, which is only 200m away from the Venue.

Subway & Bus

The Lisbon subway covers most of the city. Where the subway doesn’t reach, there’s a bus. Tickets for both are interchangeable and you can get them on vending machines on the subway. A ticket is just €1,50 (there’s a first-time 50 cent charge for the reusable card). In either case, just head to Oriente Station.
